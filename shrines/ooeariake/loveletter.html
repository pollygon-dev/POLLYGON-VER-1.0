So. I played my first ADELTA game, Ooe. I was intrigued by its premise and impressed by its art and character design which led me to playing it. Since I haven’t played Hashihime and UuultraC, this is my first vn with ADELTA :P I was supposed to wait for an official translation but I just went ahead and played it since I couldn’t wait any second longer LOL

I am blown away by how beautiful this game is. Words cannot describe how much I am moved by Kurosawa’s writing. It truly resonated with me, how she depicts human relationships in such a visceral way, how she showed each characters’ humanity and depravity, the different forms of love shown in each route, the beautiful metaphors used throughout the game… I could go on forever.

And I will NOT shut up about Ariake, I haven’t seen character writing this GOOD. He rewired my brain, like actually. Wow. I cannot get Ariake B and I will NEVER!!! GET OVER IT!!! The whole route is so captivating and tragically beautiful, and it’s super compelling watching Oosaki (EVEN ME, GODDAMN!) slowly fall into Ariake’s fatal beauty. God. The writing was so poetic in showing their feelings. I love you Ooari, my beautiful toxic doomed yaoi.

I am seriously not over Ariake, I fell in love with him the moment I finished his route. Before I knew it, he became such an important character to me… Such a beautiful and tragic character, I seriously cannot shut up about him… His beauty, his twisted view on devotion and love, his kindness despite it all… Thank you Kurosawa Rinko for making him…
