Mordred

"Wake up, Hero!"

'Mordred (Working Title)' is a turn-based RPG with a rhythm mechanic following the hero, Mordred, as he collects gems hidden away by 4 guardians to save the world.

Credits:

  • Story, Art, UI: Apollo
  • Lead Programmer: kairosolo (kairosolo on itch.io)

Characters

Mordred

He/Him

The game's protagonist. An amnesiac, he goes through the journey as an attempt to recover his memories.
