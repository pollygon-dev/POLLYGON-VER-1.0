My first step into blogging!

October 12, 2024

I finally made a blog! Took me a while to make a page for it. Or.. well. Updating my site in general because I got so busy last year during college and my daily routine became uni - go home - sleep. I was exhausted all the time. But I got to pick up HTML and CSS again so I’m glad I got the motivation to work on my neocities after a long time!

At first, I was actually debating whether I’ll do a blog or not, since I’m mostly active on twitter and I tend to just tweet out my thoughts anyways. But then, I realize that I’m sloooowly losing motivation to use that app and I just end up scrolling from time to time to see updates of stuff I’m into. I’ve probably had enough with the popular social media platforms. Then, I started writing a lot of stuff on my notepad when it comes to stuff that happens during my days or reviews of stuff I play. That’s when it hit me — Wow, I really should make a blog. LOL

I’m actually glad I finally have free time to update my site, it’s been way too long since I updated it. I’m glad I started working on the other stuff I haven’t done like: the art section, projects page, an update about me section, even a landing page! Then I remembered my rambled thoughts on my notepad, so I started working on coding a new section for my site: a blog.

So here we are, in my first blog post! I’m actually surprised at how I’m starting a blog, considering how much I hate talking about myself and keeping everything about me private HAHAHAHA. Well, I did think to myself: if I’ll talk about myself and the stuff happening around me, I might as well post it on my little corner on the internet. Here’s to me (hopefully) not forgetting to post here!
