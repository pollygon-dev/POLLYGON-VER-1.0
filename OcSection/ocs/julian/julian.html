Personality

Julian is a remarkably gentle and reserved individual, whose quiet demeanor often leads others to perceive him as distant or uninteresting. Despite these misconceptions, he possesses a sharp and analytical mind that serves him well in his role as an advisor to a noble family. His current state is marked by a devastating curse - the price of his sister's desperate decision to bring him back from death, which has left his body in a constant state of deterioration. This condition, requiring the use of a golem for mobility, has only reinforced his tendency to rely on careful thought and measured words rather than direct action.

Unlike his sister Courier's fiery temperament, Julian approaches situations with patience and careful consideration. This stark contrast with his sister's personality, however, doesn't diminish their close bond - if anything, their differences complement each other perfectly. The fact that his sister broke the sacred taboo of resurrection to save him speaks volumes about their relationship, even though it came at a terrible cost. While he may appear withdrawn to strangers, those who know him well understand that his gentle nature comes from a place of genuine kindness rather than weakness.

Even with his body's continuous deterioration due to the curse, Julian maintains his dignity and independence through his intellectual contributions. His reserved nature allows him to observe and understand situations deeply, making him particularly valuable as an advisor. Though some might mistake his quietness for timidity, Julian possesses an inner strength that shows itself in his persistence despite his curse and his compassionate understanding of his sister's fateful choice to save him, regardless of the consequences.

Background

Julian Cross is the younger brother of Courier, born in a small town between Stillmoor and Filenore. Following a devastating monster attack that claimed their parents' lives when Julian was just an infant, he and his sister found refuge among the elves of Stillmoor. While Lilian excelled in magic, Julian's body proved too weak to channel magical energy, leading him to develop his mind instead. Though quiet and reserved compared to his fiery sister, the siblings remained inseparable throughout their childhood, with Lilian raising and fiercely protecting him.

Currently serving as an advisor to a noble family, Julian's life took a darker turn when a fatal incident led his sister to break the ultimate taboo by reviving him from death. This act cursed him with a continuously deteriorating body, forcing him to rely on a golem crafted by his sister for mobility. Despite these hardships, he maintains his gentle demeanor and sharp intellect, finding purpose in his advisory role despite his physical limitations.

"Each day my body fails me is another day my sister blames herself. If only she could see that her love, even in breaking that sacred taboo, was the greatest gift I could have received."
